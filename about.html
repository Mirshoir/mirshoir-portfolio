About Me

My name is Mirshoir Rustamaliev. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sejong University, South Korea. With a deep passion for artificial intelligence and robotics, I focus on applying AI to reduce human cognitive load, support urban disaster prevention, and integrate drones and biosensors for real-world solutions.

My core research interests include geospatial machine learning and large language model (LLM) chatbots that assist with cognitive support and accessibility. I am currently building projects involving multimodal interfaces, voice assistants, and real-time flood risk simulations.

I have completed several certifications in machine learning, including:

  • Machine Learning with Python – freeCodeCamp
  • ML for Beginners – Sololearn

I speak both Korean and English and bring an international perspective to my work in AI. My long-term goal is to become a research-driven AI/ML engineer who develops innovative solutions that benefit society globally.

Outside of coding and research, I enjoy reading the works of Rumi and creating web and Android apps powered by AI.
